Note :

>> The motivation of this project is to eradicate the usage of traditional register that are hard to maintain.Instead we are trying to provide a more versatile and an online E-register to maintain student record.

>> Taking attendance of small group of students is ok but when it comes to take attendance of students in a university or institution it becomes a tedious job for faculties to do so.

>> Online Attendance System is a smart system that uses technology such as Smartphone, database and internet to mark attendance and stores its data in a excel sheet and send it to respective faculty.

>>An attempt is made to look at the prevalence in the high level of impersonation experienced on a daily basis in both private and public sectors, the ghost worker syndrome which has become a menace across all tiers of government, employers concerns over the levels of absence in their workforce and difficulty in managing student attendance during lecture periods.
